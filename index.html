Questions

Good to know.

How much does a tax return cost?

It depends on how simple or involved things are, so we'll always give you a clear quote up front before any work starts. No surprises! The easiest way to find out is to make an appointment or ask us to call you.

Do you only help Sunshine Coast clients?

Not at all! We're thrilled to serve clients in every state and territory in Australia, and overseas too. Our office is in Tewantin, and most of the work happens by phone, email and video, with a coffee in person whenever you're nearby.

Which accounting software do you support?

We're registered partners for Xero, MYOB and QuickBooks, and have worked with plenty of specialist programs over the years. If you already have a system you love, we'll happily work within it. If you're starting fresh, we'll help you choose.

What do I need to bring to my appointment?

Once you've made an appointment, we'll send you a short, plain-English checklist tailored to you, so you know exactly what to bring. No guessing, and no chasing!

Can you help with my SMSF?

Absolutely. We handle the administration, the annual compliance and the audit coordination, and we make sure you understand what you're responsible for, so the control of your super genuinely stays yours.
